Caring Hearts

2310 Smith Street

Regina, SK

S4P 2P6

1 306 523-2780

contactus@caringheartssk.ca

Caring Hearts offers a range of counselling and education programs for adults, children, youth, and families, as well as to agencies to support those struggling to cope with grief and loss. Services include in-person and virtual support. The organization also works with provincial and federal governments in support of the Missing Persons, and Missing and Murdered Indigenous Women and Girls in Saskatchewan.
