Hospice Northwest

63 Carrie Street

Thunder Bay, ON

P7A 4J2

1 807 626-5570

info@hospicenorthwest.ca

https://www.hospicenorthwest.ca/

Hospice Northwest's volunteers provide one-to-one palliative support to individuals and families in the home, hospitals, hospices, and long-term care facilities. Caregiver support is available through a 24/7 support line and a 5-week workshop. One-to-one and group grief support is offered. Services are available in English and French.

Hospice of Waterloo Region

100 Solstice Way

Waterloo, ON

N2K 0G3

1 519 743-4114

hospice@hospicewaterloo.ca

https://www.hospicewaterloo.ca/

Hospice of Waterloo Region operates the Gies Family Centre, a palliative care centre that includes the 10-bed Cook Family Residence. A range of support services for those living in the community is provided by professional counsellors and volunteers. One-to-one and group bereavement support is available, including specialized groups for Indigenous and Rainbow communities.

St. Joseph's Care Group - Hospice/Palliative Care

35 N Algoma Street 4th Floor North

Thunder Bay, ON

P7B 5G7

1 807 343-2431

sjcg@tbh.net

https://sjcg.net/services/complex-physical-rehab/inpatient/hospice.aspx

St. Joseph's Hospital has 10 hospice beds and 11 palliative care beds for adults. One-to-one grief support is offered to in-patient clients, adults, children, and families on the hospice palliative care unit. Clients also have access to Indigenous Services that include an Indigenous Counsellor, Indigenous Health Transition Coordinator, and Cultural practitioners with the Traditional Healing Program at St. Joseph's Care Group, which hosts the North West Regional Palliative Care Program

Hill House Hospice

36 Wright Street

Richmond Hill, ON

L4C 4A1

1 905 737-9308

info@hillhousehospice.com

http://www.hillhousehospice.com/

Hill House Hospice is a residential hospice that provides compassionate and expert medical care at no cost to people living with a life-limiting illness. It offers a home-like environment with private bedrooms, nutritious meals, respecting religious or dietary preferences, 24/7 medical care, high staff to resident ratio including physicians, nurses, and personal support workers, music therapy; complementary therapy including light massage, aromatherapy, reiki, and therapeutic touch. Grief and bereavement support is provided to families and caregivers.
